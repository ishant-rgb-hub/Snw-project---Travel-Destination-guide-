Essential Travel Tips

Document Preparation

Always check visa requirements and ensure your passport has at least 6 months validity before traveling.

Packing Smart

Pack light, versatile clothing and always leave some space for souvenirs. Roll clothes to save space.

Money Matters

Notify your bank of travel plans, carry some local currency, and have multiple payment options available.
